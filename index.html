WHO AM I ?

I came to the UK 14 years ago after finishing my languages studies at university in France to improve my English. I enjoyed London life to the point where I decided to stay and make a living here for myself.

After working in retail management for nearly two decades, I decided it was time I created my own company so I did not have to report to a boss anymore.


MY PROPERTY JOURNEY

I started my property journey 10 years ago with my first investment property: a studio in a student residence. I then jumped to property improvement by finding houses in need of TLC and bringing them back to their former glory.

I own several properties, bringing me a steady income every month. I now help people who are facing difficulties with their property by finding the best deal possible for both the vendor and the buyer.

MY PASSION

I am passionate about matching sellers and buyers to everyone’s satisfaction. I love getting my hands dirty refurbishing houses long forgotten about. I enjoy seeing the result of handwork and teamwork, when the houses are back to the highest standards on the market.




Why Me?

I have over 10 years’ experience in Property both for myself, my family, friends, and for several regular investors. You can benefit from my experience and my contacts in the industry, as I will help you with all the difficulties of selling and buying a house.

As I own several properties myself, I will help you avoid all the little mistakes most people make when trying to sell which could decrease the time span to completion and reduce the amount you get for your beautiful home.

My goal is to get your property sold as quickly and as stress free as possible without it costing you an arm and a leg. The idea is to bring sellers and buyers together so we all get what we want out of the deal. Making people fall in love with property again.

No Silly Offers

I promise to make reasonable offers to the sellers and not try to get a bargain. I am honest with the seller and will give the real value you can expect. The seller will not be left disappointed.

Hassle Free

I can refer a legal team to ensure an easy peasy sale. I have a several solicitors, estate agents and letting agents I work with on a regular basis from whom you can choose, if do not already have your own team. As this team is used to working together, they insure a quick completion.

Home Staging

I help stage your home to get the highest offers possible. I show how to maximize your house’s potential. Together we decide what to improve for a quick and easy sale.

Services

Here you can find an overview of why I am really the best person to help you on this property journey. This is a highlight of my services as a property matchmaker.

No Sellers Fee

As the seller, you do not need to pay me anything.I get paid by the buyer because I do all the leg work for them. Insuring that the seller gets 100% of the agreed price.

Bespoke Matchmaking

I offer your house to the best suitable buyer from the start. This will reduce the waiting time between offer and completion. Adding to the speed of it all.

Speed

I will come to visit your house within 1 week. I will call you back within 48 hours. Completion can be as quick as 7 days.

Immediate Offer

I agree with you a minimum offer under which I will not market your home to my investors on my very first visit. I will not let you hanging for weeks. I will be honest and tell you if your goal is unreasonable.

Win Win Situation

I do all the leg work for you and make sure the deal goes forward only and only when we have a win-win situation. This ensures no one is left with a sour taste at the end. You will be able to afford that new property you want.

No Strangers in your house

As I look at the houses myself, the only visitor you will have is ME. I will not bring anyone to visit your house at all hours. You will have a maximum of 3 visits from me and only if this is absolutely necessary.
